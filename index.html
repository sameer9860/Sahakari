Our Legacy of Trust

Established on April 24, 1998 (Baisakh 2055), Navodaya Cooperative was born from the vision of aware youths in our community. Our goal has always been simple yet profound: to liberate people from high-interest loans and foster a culture of saving.

Starting with just 27 individuals and NPR 13,500, we have grown into a pillar of financial stability. Today, we operate from our own concrete building in Kekha Bazaar and have expanded into agricultural initiatives, proving that cooperative efforts can lead to sustainable development.
